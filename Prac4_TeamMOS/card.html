Preparing for Exams

Without good preparation exams can be stress full. You need to plan carefully and revise systematically so that you can reduce stress. You should know when and where exam will be held, what you can take in the exam hall and how the exam will be structured.

Controlling exam stress and anxiety

It is normal to experience some level of anxiety or stress just before and during the examination. Stress is a normal reaction to unexpected events in everyday life. Thus, proper time management is extremely recommended.
